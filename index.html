Supporting patient care and research at the Mount Sinai Kravis Children’s Hospital

The Mount Sinai Children’s Center Foundation (CCF) was founded more than 30 years ago to support the children and families served by Mount Sinai Kravis Children’s Hospital. This year’s Benefit will provide critical support to exceptional junior physician-scientists conducting groundbreaking research in pediatric diseases; the Program for Underserved Children, caring for New York City’s low-income children by providing critical services that address both their medical and psychosocial needs; and the Child Life and Creative Arts Therapy Department, where children can be creative, cope with their hospitalization, and just be kids.

Thank you for joining the CCF in our commitment to improve the health and well-being of all children in the Mount Sinai community.
