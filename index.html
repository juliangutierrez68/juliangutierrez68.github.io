2022 Achievement Award Ceremony

The Icahn School of Medicine at Mount Sinai recognizes the accomplishments and contributions of its students during the Achievement Awards Ceremony. This annual ceremony is a time of celebration and highlights the MD, PhD, and Dual Degree students who have demonstrated outstanding achievements in the areas of research, leadership, diversity, social justice, and more. Watch the virtual ceremony and congratulate the graduating Class of 2022 as they embrace the next phase of their careers.
